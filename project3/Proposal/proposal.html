Invasion

The High Concept

In this game you have to protect something from objects coming in from the sides of the screen. This will be similar to the Undyne fight in Undertale.

Genre

The genre is bullet hell

Platform

Desktop

Story

The story is that you have to angle the deflector shield of a city to protect it from alien invaders

Aesthetics

The graphic style will be very simple. Bright "neon" colors for most things. The city will likely be the only exception.

Gameplay

The player will control a line and the will be able to rotate it around an objective with their mouse.
Objects will fly in from the side of the screen and the player will have to hit them to prevent them frm hitting the objective.
